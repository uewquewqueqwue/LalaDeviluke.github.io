FAQ
Questions that may have interested you.
  • Why is the bot offline?
    Answer:

    Perhaps this is caused by some kind of error that is unlikely, most likely at this moment a new command is being created for the bot or some changes are taking place, if such have occurred, you will see them on the site.

  • Why won’t the bot respond to me on my server?
    Answer:

    Please make sure the prefix has not changed, and the bot is showing online in your member list. If neither of these things are the case, please contact a support specialist for further help.
